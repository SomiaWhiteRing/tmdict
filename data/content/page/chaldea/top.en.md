CHALDEAの記録 (Chaldea Records) is a light-weight, multi-lingual compendium of 『Fate/Grand Order』 data and lore.

It is a collection of Servant and Craft Essence text and translations found in official materials, books and games. It currently does not focus on game-related stats and data.
