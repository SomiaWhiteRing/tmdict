Each of her first through third Spirit Origins are different people, but since they all originate from the same source, they COULD be considered the same person. One is coolheaded, two is tomboyish, and three acts high and mighty. Keep that in mind.
The trick to getting along with goddesses is to try to be just as serious, while also being laid back.
