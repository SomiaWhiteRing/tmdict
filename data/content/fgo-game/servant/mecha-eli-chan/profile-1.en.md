“My sense of justice is rock solid, because as you can see, I’m made of iron!”

It’s hard to tell with that steely face of hers, but she’s a very studious girl. As a noblewoman charged with the protection of the land, she tries to keep that little girl aspect of her personality locked down good and tight.
Every once in a while though, she might let slip a faint smile or a bashful look in the presence of Master, whom she trusts implicitly.
