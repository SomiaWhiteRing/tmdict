### Bond 4

#### Jaguar Punch: A

Official Skill Name: Blessing of the Jaguar
Jaguar Punch is the power of destruction.
It is the blessing given by the jaguar Divine Spirit.
You will feel no fear or pain.
A composite skill that combines the effects of Valor and Intuition.
