### Clear Interlude 「Leonidas Boot Camp」

In this game, Leonidas will gladly follow his Master’s orders and makes an excellent bodyguard.
He will be the shield to save the world. However, as much as he hates admitting it, he’s quite the “muscle brain” himself, so you need to make sure he stays in line.
