### Bond 5

#### 『La Grâce Fille Noël』

  <tr><td>Rank</td><td>A+</td></tr>

Sing Elegantly of Said Holy Birth.
The counterpart of La Grondement Du Haine.
This Noble Phantasm hymn activates when Jeanne Alter Santa Lily gets excited.
Naughty children get lectures (damage), and good children get presents (buffs).
Presents fall like snow, and it’s said to be a hit with children waiting for Christmas.
Also, there is no real need for her to sing.
