### Bond 2

Intelligent and quiet.
Peaceful, and dislikes combat.
He preaches that human love is the most important thing of all.
He sees all modern mages as the descendants of his pupils who’ve inherited his teachings, loving them equally.
…As long as the world allows that (love).
