Located in a modern-style building in a traditional section of Tokyo, the office’s motto is, “Fast, honest, and satisfactory.”

They will take any case, from investigating infidelities to searching for a lost cat, and it will be money well spent. Please stop by and inquire at your convenience.

Oh, don’t mind the woman in the black sailor’s uniform. She’s just a little off and stands out from the times.
